About f(x)

f(x) is the idea that our minds act as a function. Where a real world problem or issue comes, as it has and will always, how we react and what we do with the given circumstance.

pH Series

The series will have an exclusive branch called the 'pH' series.

  • Starts at 7 - being neutral
  • Goes up to 14 - being endurance
  • Goes down till 1 - being strength
  • Everything will be color coordinated

Qualifications for the exclusive collection

Below will be key requirements

  • Begins with everything in relation to bodyweight and a bare minimum
  • Bench press BW*1.25
  • Squat BW*1.5
  • Deadlift BW*2.0
